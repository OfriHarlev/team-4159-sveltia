<h1>IGNITE WOMEN IN STEM PANEL</h1>
<p>We partner with IGNITE to host a Women in STEM Panel every year for young women and non-binary students to learn more about STEM careers from female professionals who talk about their experiences in STEM industries as women. We&#8217;ve hosted this panel with IGNITE for the past couple of years, and are in the process of putting together the next. As a team, we value diversity and equality, and this is one of the ways our team works towards improving gender equality and promoting STEM.</p>
