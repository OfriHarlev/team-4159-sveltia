<h1><b>OUTREACH</b></h1>
<p>Through our team&#8217;s outreach, we show the youth in our community the opportunities and excitement of STEM. We strive to spread our passion for STEM throughout both our school and San Francisco by promoting creativity, teamwork, and commitment through various outreach events, including STEM nights, Ortega Library&nbsp;LEGO&nbsp;Days, and Maker Faires. At these events, we demo our robots, facilitate creative thinking through LEGO Mindstorm building, and host workshops to introduce elementary and middle school students to opportunities in the STEM field and FIRST community. Through these actions, we hope to cultivate the next generation of STEM leaders in our increasingly technological world.</p>
